My last 3 movies

Shehzada

It is a remake of tamil movie but was quite entertaining. The humor and action was pretty good.

No Smoking

IT is about smoking where the lead character is constantly smoking whenever he is awake. The starting was good, comedy was also entertaining but the ending was pure unga bunga.

Blue Beetle

Quite average DC superhero movie. Enjoyed it but not one of my top superhero films which I would recommend.
